The Platform

Built for the
thoughtful traveller

Itinerary Book is not a booking engine. It is a planning canvas — where the texture of a journey takes shape before the first bag is packed.

📖

Beautiful Itineraries

Day-by-day plans that read like a travel diary — with time, place, notes, and the space to capture the unexpected moments that make a trip.

🗺️

Live Maps

Every stop, every route, every neighbourhood — visualised on a living map that updates as your itinerary grows.

✈️

Flight & Stay Import

Forward your confirmation emails and Itinerary Book automatically builds the bones of your trip — flights, hotels, and transfers in the right order.

🤝

Collaborative Planning

Plan with your travel companions in real time. Vote on options, leave notes, and arrive on the same page — literally.

📄

Print-Ready Export

Export your itinerary as a beautifully typeset PDF — designed to be printed, bound, and carried as the travel book your journey deserves.

🌙

Offline Access

Your entire itinerary — maps, notes, bookings, and all — available without a signal. Because the best places often have the worst Wi-Fi.
